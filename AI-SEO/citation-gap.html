In the era of GEO (Generative Engine Optimization), being ranked #1 on Google is no longer the final goal. The new goal is Inclusion. If an AI (Perplexity, Gemini, ChatGPT) summarizes your industry but doesn't cite you, you have a Citation Gap.

üõ† Part 1: How to Identify Your "Citation Gap"

The Citation Gap is the delta between your organic ranking and your "Share of Voice" in AI responses.

1. The "Manual Audit" Framework (D.E.E.P)

Use the D.E.E.P. method to audit your brand manually:

  • Define: Identify 5-10 high-intent conversational prompts (e.g., "What is the best link-in-bio tool for creators?").
  • Explore: Run these across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews.
  • Evaluate: Who is the AI citing? Is it your site, a competitor, or a third-party review site like Reddit or G2?
  • Plan: If you are "Missed" (cited but not mentioned) or "Excluded," identify the source the AI did use.

2. The "Citation Share" Calculation

Calculate your visibility score using this simple formula:

Citation Share = (Queries mentioning Brand / Total AI Queries) √ó 100

Target: A healthy "Senior Analyst" benchmark is >20% for your core niche.

üéØ Part 2: The "Entity Association" Strategy

AI doesn't just crawl pages; it learns Relationships. You must associate your brand (Entity A) with established Industry Leaders (Entity B).

Strategy 1: The "Digital PR" Pivot

Stop chasing backlinks; start chasing Co-citations.

  • The Tactics: Get quoted in the same article as your biggest competitor.
  • Why: When an LLM sees "Brand X and Brand Y (Your Brand) are leading the market," it creates a permanent semantic link between you and the leader.

Strategy 2: "Entity Stacking" via Schema

Use Advanced Schema to tell the AI who your friends are.

  • KnowsAbout: Add this to your Author schema to link your brand to specific Knowledge Graph nodes (e.g., "SEO Audit").
  • SameAs: Link your website to your LinkedIn, GitHub, and high-authority directory profiles (G2, Clutch).

Strategy 3: The "Reddit/Forum" Signal

LLMs (especially ChatGPT/SearchGPT) use Reddit as a "Truth Signal."

Action: Ensure your brand is mentioned in "Best of" threads on Reddit. AI treats these unlinked mentions as high-trust citations.
