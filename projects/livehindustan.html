Project Overview

Managed SEO strategy for Livehindustan.com, India's leading Hindi news portal with 4+ million daily active users. The focus was on maintaining visibility during real-time news cycles while optimizing for Google Discover and Top Stories placement during major national events.

Challenge

News media operates at unprecedented velocity. The challenge wasn't just ranking for keywordsâ€”it was:

  • Maintaining server stability and crawlability during 10x traffic spikes (election nights, major events).
  • Optimizing real-time content for Google Discover, which rewards freshness, engagement, and topical relevance.
  • Coordinating SEO across editorial teams working in parallel on breaking stories.
  • Balancing monetization (ad placements) with user experience and SEO best practices.

Strategy & Execution

I implemented a "Newsroom SEO" framework that integrated SEO into the editorial workflow:

  • Event Planning: Pre-planned SEO campaigns for scheduled events (Elections, Asia Cup, Diwali). Created URL structures, cluster pages, and internal linking maps weeks in advance.
  • Discover Optimization: Built a scoring system to prioritize stories by Discover potential. Optimized headline structure, image metadata, and article schema.
  • Trending Module: Updated trending widgets hourly with SimilarWeb data to surface viral topics early, then guided editorial toward those angles.
  • Technical Infrastructure: Fixed crawl issues (breadcrumbs, pagination, AMP), improved Core Web Vitals, and implemented responsive design for news snippets.

Daily Responsibilities

  • Trend Monitoring (Morning): Pulled real-time data from SimilarWeb, Google Trends, and internal dashboards to identify emerging topics.
  • Editorial Guidance: Advised writers and editors on keyword optimization, headline structure, and image SEO for breaking stories.
  • Technical Audits (Weekly): Crawled site with Screaming Frog, fixed broken links, structured data, pagination issues, and mobile rendering problems.
  • Event Coverage: Managed URL redirects, bulk updates, and live blog templates for major events.
  • Reporting (Weekly): Built Looker Studio dashboards tracking ranking changes, Discover impressions, and traffic attribution.

Results & Impact

Over 2+ years, the combined strategy delivered:

  • 4M+ Daily Active Users: Maintained consistent visibility and engagement during major news cycles without search ranking drops.
  • 70%+ Discover Optimization Rate: Over 70% of published stories now appear in Google Discover or Top Stories within 2 hours of publication.
  • 100K+ Page 1 Keywords: Built a sustainable keyword portfolio across news, entertainment, sports, and opinion content.
  • Event-Based Growth: Election coverage, sports tournaments, and festivals consistently drove 2-3x traffic spikes with optimized user experience.

Key Learnings

  • News SEO is about speed, freshness, and trust. Ranking authority comes from real-time relevance, not just backlinks.
  • Discover rewards sites that publish diverse, visual content with strong engagement signals. Focus on CTR and time-on-page, not just rankings.
  • Coordination between SEO and editorial teams must be baked into workflow. Ad-hoc SEO guidance doesn't scale at high velocity.
  • Technical stability under load is a feature, not an afterthought. A slow site loses Discover visibility and rankings during traffic spikes.
